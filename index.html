Observations from Real Testing

Image Sizing & Contrast

  • Bright daylight testing correctly classified skin undertones as Warm Olive (Profile 24), suggesting a rich Deep Autumn palette.

Lighting Weaknesses

  • Low-light images misclassified skin undertones as Cool (Profile 40), suggesting the vision layers lack exposure normalization.

Indian Skin Calibration

  • Mapping 42 distinct skin profiles is far superior to Western 12-season systems which misclassify deeper tones.

Cohesiveness Risks

  • The fashion engine occasionally suggested mismatched items (e.g. formal blazer + streetwear cargo) due to broad styling instructions.

UI/UX Recommendations

1
Normalize Image Inputs

Add front-end guides to ensure users take photos under natural light, and run contrast normalization on image pixels before analysis.

2
Dynamic Sizing Validation

Enforce strict clothing categories in prompt configurations so outfit outputs are complete and visually balanced.
